    How to play Just One

    Team up to help the guesser find the secret word using unique clues.

    1. Join the table. Choose a name, select Guesser or Hint giver, then tap Join.
    2. Start a round. A hint giver starts once the room has at least one guesser and one hint giver.
    3. Write one clue. Hint givers see the word and submit a single-word clue. Proper nouns or multi-word clues are blocked in hard mode.
    4. Remove duplicates. Hint givers compare their clues and eliminate any duplicates or collisions before revealing them. A collision happens when one clue is a direct description of another (for example, if the clue is "actor", then "person" is not allowed because an actor is a person; "Ryan Reynolds" is not allowed because he is a actor). Hint givers talk it out and remove similar or related words before the clues are shown.
    5. Make the guess. The guesser sees the remaining clues and submits an answer. Everyone wins or loses together.
    6. Keep playing. Start the next round right away or adjust settings between rounds for a new challenge.

    Need a refresher later? Click the question mark icon any time.
